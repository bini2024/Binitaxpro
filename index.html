Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for tax filing?
You will need your income statements, receipts for deductions, and any other relevant financial documents.
How can I maximize my tax refund?
Consider all deductions you qualify for, and ensure you are using all available credits.
What if I owe taxes?
If you owe taxes, it’s important to file on time to avoid penalties and consider setting up a payment plan.
How long does the tax filing process take?
Typically, it can take anywhere from a few days to a couple of weeks, depending on your situation.
